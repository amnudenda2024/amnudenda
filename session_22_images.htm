Session_22_Reading of All Words
Saturday 17th March 2012_3.30-6.30pm

at ICA, London (link)
The Mall, SW1Y 5AH

Reading of All Words is a reading by two actors of the Am Nuden Da project Words.

The reading is a development of an ongoing project in which 85 Artists were invited to email a text work to Am Nuden Da. The resulting exhibition has been shown at their project space in East London and at Four Boxes Gallery, Krabbesholm, Denmark. The works have now been compiled into book form and to coincide with the Publish and Be Damned Fair (link), there will be a reading of the complete publication.

The book will be launched on this occasion and will be available at the ICA bookshop.

To purchase the publication online please visit Motto. (link)

Words + Untitled is published by Krabbesholm Højskole, Skive, Denmark. (link)

The reading will begin at 3.30pm and last approximately 3 hours.
