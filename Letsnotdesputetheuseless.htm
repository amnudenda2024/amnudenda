Let's Not Dispute The Useless. ... Gerry Bibby, Juliette Blightman, Kerstin Brätsch, Oliver Laric, Gil Leung




Let's Not Dispute The Useless. … is the working title for five solo exhibitions by Gerry Bibby, Juliette Blightman, Kerstin Brätsch, Oliver Laric and Gil Leung. At the invitation of Am Nuden Da each artist will realise an exhibition at a location of the artists’ choosing. The exhibitions will be visited by Am Nuden Da and documented through a daylong conversation with the artist. The resulting accounts will be published as a book, making the exhibitions available by way of agreed-on-fictions.
